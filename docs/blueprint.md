# **App Name**: UECD Official Portal

## Core Features:

- Informative Home Page: A home page to present key highlights, mission, and statistics of UECD.
- Treaty and Legal Framework Repository: Centralized repository for treaties and legal documents with online reading and PDF download options.
- Governance Structure Overview: Detailed explanation of UECD's governance bodies, their functions, and operational processes.
- Economic Integration Timeline: A visual timeline and diagrams to illustrate the stages and roadmap of economic integration within UECD.
- Sector-Specific Policy Pages: Dedicated pages for each sector (energy, industry, agriculture, etc.) outlining policy objectives, programs, and regulations.
- Interactive Data Dashboards: Interactive data dashboards presenting annual reports, economic indicators, and policy assessments with downloadable data.
- Multilingual AI Translation Tool: Tool powered by generative AI to offer content translation, to broaden international engagement.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Strong Blue (#3778C2), evokes trust and stability.
- Background color: Light blue (#E8F0FE), to maintain a professional yet modern aesthetic.
- Accent color: Darker Blue (#25316D), to add depth.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif, for a clean, professional, neutral feel that ensures readability.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' serif, for an elegant, intellectual, contemporary feel for headers.
- Consistent use of minimalist icons to represent different sections and functions.
- Clean and minimal design with generous use of white space to enhance clarity.
